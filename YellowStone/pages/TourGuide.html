Yellowstone

Oozing Magma, Fun for the Whole Family

Welcome! This will be a quick guide to all the major places to visit when you are at Yellowstone National Park.

Firstly the most common spot is Old Faithful which is one of the larger gysers in the park that erupts around every 90 minutes give or take 20 minutes.

Second is Mammoth Hot Springs. Mammoth is hope to some of the weirdest views of the park where alien like landscapes just seem to erupt from the ground. It is filled with lots of intresting whites, grays, and oranges. Another less imprresive feature is the smell that is produced by the springs.

Third is Grand Prismatic. Grand Prismatic is one of most colorful and large springs the park has to offer. If you do visit the spring make sure to take the hike up to the overlook for a unique view from above.

Fourth is Grand Teton National Park located just outside of Yellowstone. The Tetons are some of the largest mountains at Yellowstone. The Tetons also are home to Yellowstone lake which is also a very pretty view.

Finally for this guide is the Grand Canyon of Yellowstone. The Grand Canyon is most peoples favorite place to visit because of it's scale a beauty. The canyon includes Yellowstone River which flows through many amazing waterfalls.

Please do not only visit these spots there are many amazing features of the park that there is to see.
