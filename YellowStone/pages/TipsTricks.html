Tips & Tricks

  1. Be prepared to spend alot of time in the car!
  2. Bring binoculars because most of the wildlife you will see will be quite a distance
  3. If there are alot of cars stopped and you see a crowd of people most likely they are looking at something pretty rare
  4. There are a few guided Yellowstone tour apps that use you location to give you a tour of park real time while you drive around
  5. Almost all the park has no signal so if you need to access the internet or text someone make sure you do it before you enter the park
  6. If you want to enjoy the park in more of a personal experience many of the longer hikes will allow you to get away from all the car tourists
  7. Bring enough food and water to last you the day because there are only a few restruants in the park and it takes a while to get around.
  8. Make sure you know the road closings before you go because some of the places there is only one road to get there
  9. Brush up on your parking skills because it will be a challenge to find a spot and you need to be ready when you find one
  10. Don't focus to much on just Yellowstone when you visit the sournding area also have lots places to explore
  11. MOST IMPORTANT TIP: The earlier you get there the faster you will get through the gate and find a parking spot at the major locations
  12. Be careful and check the fire conditions because currently the area is coverd in them
  13. My favorite places were always the less traveled ones find pullofs nobody else is taking and just have a look around it also helps to strech your legs
