Pretty Pictures

Wolf walking out of the forest. Shot from the side of the road using a telephoto lens to get a closer look.

Moose walking on the side of the road.

Family of Bison. This image had pretty good framing to show what a image that can portray the story of the bison just roaming the land. They are incredibly beutiful creatures which have the appearence of dinosaurs when you first see them

Sunset shot on the shore of Henry's Lake State Park. First time shooting a sunset picture using a long shutterspeed to capture the proper amount of light to ensure sharpness.

Grand Canyon of Yellowstone Waterfall. This was my favorite part of the trip the scale of the Canyon was amazing and the waterfall was stunning. First place I've seen in a while that actually took my breath away.

Fairy Falls Waterfall. First hike we did ended up being about 6 miles total which was alot for a hot day. Super worth the trek though because the falls was one of the highlights of my trip.

Yellowstone Lake. Foggy day allowed my to capture this mysterious picture not one of the most flashy of the trip but I think it ended up being one of my favorite because of the creative composition.

Grand Teton National Park right next to Yellowstone. Long 4 hour drive there and back to see the Tetons it was rainy which was pretty unlucky but it is still an amazing sight to see.

Grand Prismatic Spring. The thermal vents allow microbes to grow which gives it's amazing color. Grand Prismatic size and color make it extremly photogenic.

Mammoth Springs. Mammoth Springs is an otherworldy experience the strange colors and dead vegitation makes it an alien landscape when combined with the steam produced by the springs. The whole place is also coverd in the smell of rotton eggs.
