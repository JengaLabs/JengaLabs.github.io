Pig's Vault

  • Party Pig Games

    With the long term goal of being a collection of party games to play with freinds. Party Pig Games is my first Unity Project after my hiatias over the summer. With the intention to ensure the project is finished. The first game to be added to the collection had to be "simple".

  • First Game

    A recent obseion of mine is watching Wheel of forturne games played by the Game Grumps. Watching each other both guess the words that couldn't possibly be right is comedy gold. However there are plenty of points watching where the words displayed don't match the category. Or just a lack of modern words to guess made the first game clear to make.

  • Features 1

    The first hurdle I wanted to tackle was to ensure I knew what features the game needed to have ahead of time. Playing with freinds online and being able to let others download the game is big feature. However I do not want every player to only have access to the only words I put in. The ability to extend the categorys and words inside them I a big feature that would definitly help the game. Finally having sounds and fun UI is vital to making these games enjoyable.

  • Features 2

    While I don't have much expeirence in making UI visualy apealing in Unity. That could be the last hurdle to making the game. As effects will come last over funciontality.
