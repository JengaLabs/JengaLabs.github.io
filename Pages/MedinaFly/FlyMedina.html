About Medina Municipal Airport 1G5

Constructued in 1957 by Mr. Robert Freed and opperated privately till 1987 when it was bought by the City of Medina and became the Medina Municipal Airport. The same year, the FAA designated the airport as a reliever airport for general aviation air traffic for Cleveland Hopkins International Airport.
The city has entered into an operating agreement with Flight Services of Medina for the day-to-day operation of the airport. Flight Services of Medina provide aircraft maintence, rental and flight intstructions.

Flight Information

The airport is located 4 miles east of the City of Medina on a 290 acre parcel of land and is home to nearly 90 based aircraft, many of which are used corporately.

  • Traffic Pattern: Left
